Fluso is an AI assistant that works **inside your tools**, not beside them. You connect the apps you already use — Gmail, Google Calendar, Slack, GitHub, and others — and Fluso manages the work across them through plain English conversation.

It is not a chatbot. It reads your inbox, drafts replies, schedules meetings, processes transcripts, writes documents, generates code, runs research, and remembers everything — so you spend your time on the work that matters.

## The problem Fluso solves

You spend hours every week on work *about* work:

- Triaging email — 30 to 60 minutes a day.
- Finding meeting times and sending invites — 15 to 30 minutes per meeting.
- Taking notes and writing follow-ups — 30 to 45 minutes per meeting.
- Drafting documents, decks, and spreadsheets from a blank page.
- Researching across 15 browser tabs and stitching findings together.
- Switching between six apps to piece together a single answer.

Fluso handles these so you can focus elsewhere.

## What Fluso does

| Instead of... | Fluso does this |
|---|---|
| Scanning 50 unread emails every morning | "Summarise my inbox" — Fluso tells you what needs action, what is FYI, what is noise. |
| Manually creating tasks from emails | Auto-detects requests in incoming mail and creates tracked tasks with deadlines. |
| Staring at a blank Google Doc | "Write a project proposal with executive summary, timeline, and budget." Done. |
| Digging through three months of Slack | "What did the team decide about pricing?" Fluso finds the answer. |
| Reading a two-hour meeting transcript | Delivers a summary, decisions, action items, and follow-up drafts. |
| Googling and reading 15 tabs | Researches the topic with citations and produces a structured report. |

### Write and review code

Connect GitHub and Fluso becomes a development partner. It browses codebases, reviews pull requests, scaffolds projects from a description, finds bugs with file-and-line references, and generates onboarding tours.

### Remember everything

Every email, meeting, document, and Slack thread builds your personal knowledge graph — people, projects, topics, decisions. Ask "what did we decide about the Q2 launch?" and Fluso finds the answer across months of context.

## Who Fluso is for

- **Executives and founders** drowning in email and meetings — Fluso triages, preps, follows up, and tracks every action item.
- **Developers** who want to stay in flow — Fluso reviews PRs, fixes bugs, scaffolds projects, and answers questions about your codebase.
- **Product managers and researchers** who synthesise — Fluso runs deep research with citations and turns findings into decks.
- **Teams** that need shared context — Fluso captures decisions and keeps everyone aligned without extra status meetings.
- **Anyone who creates content** — turn a description into a finished doc, deck, image, or web page in minutes.
