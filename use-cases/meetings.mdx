A complete walkthrough of running a meeting end-to-end with Fluso. Save 45–60 minutes per meeting on prep and post-work.

## A real Tuesday

It's 9:00 AM. You have a project review meeting with the Acme team at 10am. You haven't prepped.

### Without Fluso

Scramble through emails. Find the last meeting notes. Check Slack for Acme discussions. Try to remember what decisions were pending. Arrive at 10am feeling unprepared.

After the meeting: 20 minutes typing notes → 15 minutes creating tasks → 15 minutes writing follow-up email → 10 minutes updating Slack. Done by 11:45.

### With Fluso

#### 9:55 — Prep (1 minute)

> *"Prep me for the 10am Acme meeting."*

Fluso delivers:

- **Attendees:** Sarah (VP Product), Mark (CTO), your team (James, Lisa).
- **Last meeting (April 18):** Agreed Phase 1 scope, contract pending Legal.
- **Since then:** 3 emails from Sarah about pilot expansion, Slack discussion in #partnerships about timeline.
- **Open items:** Legal review overdue (your action item), technical specs due Friday (James).
- **Likely topics:** Pilot expansion timeline, API rate limiting concerns, Q3 budget.

#### 10:00 — Meeting

You focus on the conversation. Press record in Zoom.

#### 10:45 — Process (1 minute)

> *"Process this meeting transcript."*

Fluso delivers:

| Output | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Summary** | Team agreed to expand pilot to 50 users. Timeline: May 25. Legal review must complete first. |
| **6 tasks** | With assignees and deadlines. |
| **Follow-up email** | Ready to send to all attendees. |
| **Slack update** | Ready for #partnerships. |

#### 10:47 — Done

Meeting processed. Tasks tracked. Follow-ups drafted. Everyone aligned.

**~85 minutes saved per meeting.** Multiply by your weekly meeting load.

## Variations

### When you weren't in the meeting

> *"Process this meeting recording — I wasn't there. Catch me up on what was decided."*

Fluso skips the personalised follow-up draft and gives you a comprehensive briefing.

### Recurring meetings

> *"Process this 1:1 transcript and update Lisa's profile in my knowledge graph."*

Each meeting compounds — your context on each person and project gets richer.

### Async meetings

> *"Process this Loom recording and create tasks from the action items."*

Works for any video or audio source, not just live calls.

## Meeting prep — without an agenda

> *"Prep me for my next 3 meetings today."*

Fluso processes them in batch. You review them all over coffee.
