## A few honest notes

The first week, none of these will feel as smooth as they read. Fluso is learning your contacts, your projects, your tone. Day three is noticeably better than day one; week two is when most of these flip from useful to indispensable.

You don't have to do all of them. The compound value of multiple integrations is real, but trying to install seven new habits at once is how nothing sticks. Pick the one that fixes the loudest pain. Run it for a week. Then add the next.

The patterns generalise. None of these are special prompts you have to memorise. The shape — describe the outcome, get the outcome, review what comes back — is the same across all of them.
