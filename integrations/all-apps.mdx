Fluso connects to a hundred-plus services. The most-used ones are below. If something you need isn't here, [tell us](mailto:support@premai.io) — new connectors get added regularly, prioritised by what people ask for.

Every connector uses OAuth2 and follows the same security model. The full picture is in [Security & privacy](/resources/security).
