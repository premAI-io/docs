Calendar pairs naturally with Gmail. Together they enable the morning brief, meeting prep, and the full meeting workflow. On its own it still does enough to be worth connecting on day one.

## What changes once it's on

The full feature page is at [Calendar & meetings](/features/calendar). The short version: see your schedule with context, find time without playing Tetris, schedule meetings from chat, and have Fluso prep you before a call. Tasks with deadlines fold into the calendar view.

## Setup

Open **Settings → Connectors** and click **Connect** on the Google Calendar card. The Google OAuth flow opens. If you already authorised Gmail with the same account, this is even shorter.

Permissions:

- **Read events** — to see your schedule and check availability.
- **Create and modify events** — to schedule meetings on your behalf.

Without write access the morning brief still works, but scheduling from chat doesn't.

## Prompts that work well

Schedule:

> *"What's on my calendar today?"*

> *"What does my week look like?"*

> *"Show me my meetings with Acme this month."*

Find time:

> *"When am I free for thirty minutes tomorrow?"*

> *"Find a one-hour slot this week when the engineering team is available."*

> *"I need two hours of focus time today."*

Schedule from chat:

> *"Schedule a thirty-minute standup tomorrow at 9am."*

> *"Create a meeting with Sarah next Monday at 2pm."*

> *"Set up a recurring 1:1 with my direct reports every other Tuesday."*

Prep:

> *"Prep me for my 3pm."*

> *"Brief me on my next meeting with Acme."*

## Disconnecting

In **Settings → Connectors**, click **Disconnect** on the Google Calendar card. Tokens are deleted immediately.

You can also revoke from [Google Account → Security → Third-party apps](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A calendar I expected isn't showing up">
    Make sure the calendar is shared to the account you authorised. Fluso only sees calendars visible to that Google account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Times look off by hours">
    Confirm your timezone in Fluso settings matches your Google Calendar timezone. Mismatches shift event times.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't create events">
    Verify the **Create and modify events** permission is granted. Reconnect if the original prompt only asked for read access.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
