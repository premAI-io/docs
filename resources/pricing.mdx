Three plans. The Free plan is enough to decide whether Fluso works for you. Pro is most people's home. Team adds the things that matter when several people are using it together. Enterprise exists if you need custom infrastructure or compliance.

## Plans

    - Full chat access
    - 3 connected apps

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans anytime?">
    Yes. Upgrade or downgrade from your account settings. Upgrading is immediate. Downgrading keeps your data but premium features go off at the next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a Pro trial?">
    Yes. Fourteen days, no card. Full Pro access, then you decide.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What counts as a message?">
    A message you send and the response that follows count as one. Follow-ups in the same thread count as separate messages on Free; on Pro and Team, messages are unlimited.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens at Free limits?">
    You're notified when you're getting close. Upgrade to keep going without interruption. Your data is preserved either way.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="More storage or apps on Pro?">
    Pro is 5GB and 10 apps. Team starts at 20GB and 25 apps per user. For unusual needs, [contact us](mailto:sales@premai.io) about Enterprise.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Startup or nonprofit discounts?">
    Yes. [Email us](mailto:sales@premai.io) and tell us about your organisation. We're happy to work something out.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Free vs Pro skills?">
    Free includes the document-shaped skills: PDFs, Word, presentations, spreadsheets. Pro adds image generation, deep research with citations, meeting intelligence, video summaries, and the rest.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cancellation?">
    Anytime. Cancel from your account settings. Pro access continues until the end of the billing period, then reverts to Free. Your data stays.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
