## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans anytime?">
    Yes. Upgrade or downgrade from your account settings. Upgrade is immediate. Downgrade keeps your data but removes premium features at the next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for Pro?">
    Yes. 14-day free trial of Pro. No credit card required. You get full access to everything in Pro for two weeks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What counts as a message?">
    Each user message and the response that follows count as one message. Follow-ups in the same thread count as separate messages on the Free plan. On Pro and Team, messages are unlimited.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens at Free plan limits?">
    You'll be notified when approaching the limit. Upgrade to Pro to continue without interruption. Your data is preserved.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I add more storage or apps to Pro?">
    Pro includes 5GB and 10 apps. Team plans start at 20GB and 25 apps per user. For custom needs, contact us about Enterprise.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer startup or nonprofit discounts?">
    Yes. Email [sales@premai.io](mailto:sales@premai.io) and tell us about your organisation. We're happy to work something out.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Free vs Pro skills?">
    Free includes core skills: PDFs, Word docs, presentations, spreadsheets. Pro adds advanced skills: AI image generation, deep research with citations, meeting intelligence, video summaries, and more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Cancel from your account settings. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period, then revert to Free. Your data is preserved.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
