## What is Fluso?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What exactly is Fluso?">
    Fluso is an AI assistant that connects to your apps — Gmail, Google Calendar, Slack, GitHub, and 100+ more — and helps you manage work through natural conversation. It reads your email, drafts replies, manages your calendar, creates documents, researches topics, writes code, and remembers everything.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is Fluso just another chatbot?">
    No. A chatbot answers questions. Fluso performs actions — reads and sends emails, manages your calendar, creates files, writes code, executes commands. It works across your tools, not just inside a chat window.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is Fluso different from ChatGPT or Claude?">
    ChatGPT and Claude are general-purpose AI assistants in a chat window. Fluso connects directly to your tools, builds a personal knowledge graph about your work, takes action across apps, and remembers everything. It's an executive assistant, not a conversational AI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who is Fluso for?">
    Anyone who spends too much time on email, meetings, research, content creation, or context-switching between tools. Executives, founders, developers, product managers, researchers, and teams.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does setup take?">
    About 5 minutes. Create an account (30 seconds), connect Gmail (2 clicks), and start using it. The more apps you connect, the more useful Fluso becomes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which apps should I connect first?">
    Gmail and Google Calendar. They give you the most immediate value. Add Slack if your team uses it. Add GitHub if you're a developer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need technical skills?">
    No. Fluso is designed for everyone. You interact in plain English. If you're a developer, you can use it for code — but you don't need any technical background for email, calendar, tasks, or content.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get the most out of Fluso?">
    Connect 2–3 apps, use it daily for a week, process a meeting transcript, try creating a document or presentation, and create projects for major work areas. Fluso compounds with use.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using Fluso

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What can I ask Fluso to do?">
    Anything related to your connected tools and workspace. Summarise your inbox, draft replies, manage your calendar, research topics, create documents and decks, write and review code, analyse data, process meeting transcripts. If it involves your digital work, Fluso can probably help.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does Fluso know what to do?">
    Fluso understands your request in context. It knows your connected apps, projects, preferences, and conversation history. It picks the right tools — searching email, reading files, calling APIs, generating content — to fulfil your request.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can Fluso handle multiple tasks at once?">
    Yes. *"Summarise my unread emails, check my calendar for tomorrow, and draft a reply to the client"* — Fluso handles all three in sequence.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if Fluso doesn't understand?">
    It asks clarifying questions: *"Which project?"* or *"What format?"* Answer the question for a better result.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use Fluso on my phone?">
    Yes. Fluso is a web app that works on any device with a browser. There's also a desktop app for Mac and Windows. A mobile app is in development.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Fluso work in other languages?">
    Fluso works primarily in English but can understand and respond in many languages. Quality varies by language and task complexity.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can Fluso see all my emails?">
    Fluso reads emails when you ask it to (e.g., "summarise my inbox") and as part of auto-task extraction. It doesn't store copies permanently or share them with anyone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Fluso send emails without my permission?">
    Never. Fluso always shows a draft and asks for approval before sending.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where is my data stored?">
    In your isolated workspace on encrypted infrastructure. Files, conversations, tasks, and the knowledge graph are private to you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I delete my data?">
    Yes. Delete individual knowledge graph entries, disconnect apps (tokens destroyed), or delete your entire account. All data is permanently removed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Fluso train on my data?">
    No. Your private data is never used to train AI models.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Stop using a connected app?">
    Disconnect it from Settings → Connectors. Tokens are deleted immediately. You can also revoke from the app itself (Google Account, Slack workspace, GitHub apps).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

For full details, see [Security & privacy](/resources/security).

## Skills & capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are skills?">
    Skills are specialised capabilities — creating PDFs, generating images, analysing meetings, researching topics. They activate automatically when you ask for something they can do.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to install skills?">
    No. Fluso detects relevant skills automatically based on your request.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I create my own skills?">
    Yes. Pro and Team users can create custom skills by adding a `SKILL.md` file to their workspace. Skills define workflows, tools, and outputs for specific tasks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Free vs Pro skills?">
    Free includes core skills: documents, presentations, spreadsheets. Pro adds advanced skills: AI image generation, deep research with citations, meeting intelligence, video summaries, and more.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Fluso isn't finding my emails. What's wrong?">
    Verify Gmail is connected (Settings → Connectors). Try disconnecting and reconnecting. If the issue persists, you may have hit a Gmail API rate limit — wait a few minutes and try again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fluso's response seems off. What should I do?">
    Start a new thread (click **+**) if the conversation has drifted. You can also say *"forget the context so far and start fresh on this topic."* Being more specific in your request also helps.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I connected an app but Fluso says it's not connected.">
    Try refreshing the page or reconnecting the app. OAuth tokens can expire — go to Settings → Connectors and click **Reconnect**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    Email [support@premai.io](mailto:support@premai.io). Pro users get priority email support. Team users get priority chat support. Enterprise customers get dedicated support.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
